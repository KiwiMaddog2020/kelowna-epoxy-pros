Process

The prep is the job.

The finished floor gets the attention, but the prep is where the floor earns it. Every quote should start with the concrete, not a colour chart.

  1. 01

    Inspect The Slab

    Look at cracking, moisture risk, slope, existing coatings, drains, and edge details.

  2. 02

    Prepare The Surface

    Grind, clean, patch, and profile the concrete so the coating has a proper mechanical bond.

  3. 03

    Build The Coating

    Install the system in clean stages, from base coat and flake to topcoat and cure guidance.

  4. 04

    Hand Off The Floor

    Leave the space tidy and explain when to walk, park, move items, and clean.
